On the Water

Surfing & the Sea

Kathaluwa surf break is 150 metres from the beach villas — one of the most consistent point breaks on Sri Lanka's southern coast. The reef directly in front of the beach villas creates calm, clear conditions perfect for swimming and snorkelling. Colourful fish and reef life are visible just metres from the shore.

Popular breaks at Ahangama, Weligama and Mirissa are all within 10 minutes. We can arrange boards, lessons, and guides. From October to April, traditional fishing boats are available from Kathaluwa for deep-sea trolling, guided by local fishermen who have worked these waters for generations.

Mind & Body

Yoga & Ayurvedic Wellness

Morning yoga sessions with experienced instructors, set against the rhythm of the Indian Ocean. We arrange everything — you arrive and breathe. Sessions are tailored to your level and preference, whether you're a daily practitioner or trying for the first time.

Discover traditional Ayurvedic wellness with massages, herbal treatments and personalised therapies from expert Sri Lankan practitioners. These are not spa treatments imported from abroad — they are the authentic healing traditions of this island, offered in the privacy of your villa.

Sri Lankan Cuisine

Cooking Demonstrations

Learn the art of authentic Sri Lankan cuisine with our in-house chefs. From spice grinding to coconut milk curries, from pol sambol to hoppers — a lesson in the flavours that make Sri Lanka extraordinary.

Our chefs source ingredients locally — fish from the morning catch, spices from the nearby tea and cinnamon plantations, fresh coconuts from the property. You cook, you eat, and you take the recipes home. This is a meal that takes three hours and lasts a lifetime.

Sri Lankan Culture

Cultural Evenings

Arrange traditional Kandyan dancers, drummers and ceremonial performers on the beach or in the villa gardens. A Sri Lankan cultural evening — fire jugglers, traditional costumes, the hypnotic rhythm of the double-headed drums — is unlike anything in a hotel ballroom.

Fireworks over the Indian Ocean for celebrations. Full moon beach dinners. Lantern releases. Every cultural experience is arranged through our local network and coordinated by the villa manager to unfold seamlessly during your stay.
