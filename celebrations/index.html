The Setting

Three Villas. One Estate. The Indian Ocean.

South Point Ocean, South Point Villa and South Point Abbey are adjacent beachfront villas whose gardens connect. Combined, they create a private estate on the Indian Ocean accommodating 38 or more guests across three private pools and 18 bedrooms.

The beach is yours. No other guests. No strangers. Every room reserved for your party, every staff member focused entirely on your event.

Ceremonies can take place on the beach, in the villa gardens, or under the centuries-old trees of the Abbey courtyard. Dinners are served alfresco by the ocean. Fireworks light up the sea after dark.

Occasions We Love

Every Celebration, Perfectly Placed

Destination Weddings

An intimate ceremony on the sand, a feast by the ocean, and a night of dancing under the stars. We coordinate every detail — flowers, catering, musicians, officiant, and the fireworks that end the evening — so you are present for every moment.

Milestone Birthdays

A 40th, 50th or 60th that your guests will talk about for the rest of their lives. Three villas, one estate, a private beach, and Sri Lanka's warmth. The owner plans personally — no event coordinator, no middleman.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Reunions

Bring the whole family — from grandparents to grandchildren. Children love the animal farm. Adults love the pools. Everyone loves the food. We accommodate families of 20 to 38 guests with patience, warmth, and experience.

Christmas & New Year

Escape winter for a Christmas on the beach. Turkey by the ocean. Sri Lankan midnight festivities. Selected villas are available over the festive season — enquire early, these dates are taken quickly.

Corporate Retreats

A leadership offsite unlike any conference room. Work in the morning, swim in the afternoon, dine together at sunset. Our villas offer complete privacy, reliable WiFi, and the kind of environment that produces real conversations.

Group Getaways

Friends who have been meaning to holiday together for years. Book South Point Ocean for up to 30 guests, or mix and match villas for smaller groups with their own private space. We make it work.
