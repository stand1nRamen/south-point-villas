Beachfront · Ahangama

South Point Villa

South Point Villa was designed by Murad Ismail — a protégé of Geoffrey Bawa, Sri Lanka's most celebrated architect and a figure revered across the world of tropical modernism. The result is a villa that breathes with its setting: open-sided living areas, seamless transitions between inside and out, four-poster beds draped in mosquito netting, and a quiet elegance that feels both timeless and effortlessly contemporary.

Three spacious bedrooms, each with ensuite bathroom, look directly over the Indian Ocean. The villa includes a private pool on the beachfront, walk-in kitchen, and a dedicated team of staff who have worked with the family for years.
