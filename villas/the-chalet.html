The Chalet

2 Bedroom Private Villa — One Minute from Kathaluwa Beach

Inland · Kathaluwa

The Chalet

The Chalet is the most intimate villa in the South Point collection — two beautifully appointed bedrooms with private pool, designed for couples, honeymoons, and small families who want absolute privacy without the scale of a larger estate.

One minute's walk takes you to Kathaluwa beach, where the surf breaks consistently from November through April. The Chalet sits next to South Point Surf House, making the two a natural pairing for groups who need four to seven bedrooms across adjacent properties. Inland guests have complimentary access to the beachfront villas when they are unoccupied.
